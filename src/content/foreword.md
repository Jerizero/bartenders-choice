---
title: WELCOME TO BARTENDER'S CHOICE
---

The following guide is meant to mimic the experience of sitting in front of a talented, passionate bartender willing to make anything you so desire. We have been compiling and tweaking these recipes, with the help of many passionate co-workers, every time we set foot behind a bar.

You will find a very concise and detailed list of classic cocktails as well as modern recipes from amazing bartenders from around the world. I have to stress that this is not intended to be a historical guide providing the first printed recipes for certain cocktails. Each recipe has been trialed over and over again and tweaked to appeal to the modern palate and a broader audience. It is our interpretation of the recipe. Much like any recipe (food or drink), it comes down to what you like. The recipes in the guide do seem to have a pretty high rate of satisfaction, but feel free to tweak them much like we have.

This guide is intended to be for enjoyment purposes only, be it at your home or bar/restaurant. We hope you enjoy making and drinking these drinks as much as we have over the years.

Sam Ross
