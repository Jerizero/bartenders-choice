### JUICES
Always juice fresh! Always juice the day of. You can store these fresh juices in the fridge but remember they will start oxidizing immediately. Never use pre-juiced/bottled juices, you will notice a large difference in the quality of the end result.

### SIMPLE SYRUP
1:1. This means use equal parts white granulated sugar to hot water and stir/shake until fully dissolved. Store in fridge. Should last 7-10 days.

Note: You do not need to prepare on a stove using a flame, this is a myth.

### HONEY
Mix 3 parts honey with one part HOT water. Example for 1 liter mix 24 oz honey and 8 oz water. Stir thoroughly and store in glass bottle. Rinse outside of bottle and store.

### GINGER
Juice roughly chopped ginger root (skin on) through a centrifugal juicer (Breville). Stir white granulated sugar in at a rate of 4:3 (4 parts fresh ginger juice to 3 parts granulated sugar). Stir until sugar is completely dissolved. Store in fridge when not in use. Should last 3-4 days.

### CURACAO
Mix equal parts of orange brandy or rum based liquor and simple syrup. (i.e. 200mls liquor to 200mls simple syrup). 

### GRENADINE
Add 8oz pomegranate concentrate to 1.25 liters of simple syrup and 2oz of rose water
