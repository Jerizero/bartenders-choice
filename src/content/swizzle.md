---
title: How to Swizzle
image: swizzle.png
---

Works best when you use crushed ice. Pour drink ingredients into vessel and fill 2/3s with crushed ice, dash bitters (if required) and insert bar spoon down to the layer that needs mixing (bitters, mint, berries, etc). Churn ice and mixture by running hands along the coil part of the bar spoon in opposite directions.  Churn or swizzle about 10 times and top with crushed ice. Add garnish \n and straws.
