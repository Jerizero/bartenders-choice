---
title: How to Stir
image: stir.png
---

Use a Boston glass straight out of the freezer and add ingredients first. Then fill to the brim with cracked ice (small and large pieces). Stir so as to create as little separation as possible. This is easily achieved by keeping the back of the bar spoon touching the inside of the glass at all times. Try to use only your fingers when you stir, your wrist should remain still. (this takes practice!)
